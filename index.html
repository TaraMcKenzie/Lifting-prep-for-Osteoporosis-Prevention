Build Your Lifting Strength

Lesson: The Foundation of Strength

This lesson focuses on creating a strong, stable foundation for all your lifting activities. By specifically targeting the core system, which includes the pelvic floor and the muscles that stabilize the shoulders, we can improve our ability to lift heavier weights safely and effectively.

Key Objectives:

  • Understand the Core System: Learn what the core system is and why it's more than just your abs.

  • Pelvic Floor Activation: Discover how to properly engage your pelvic floor to create intra-abdominal pressure, a key component for spinal stability.

  • Shoulder Girdle Stability: Strengthen the muscles around your shoulder blades to prevent injury and create a solid base for overhead lifts.

  • Integrated Movement: Learn how to combine pelvic floor and shoulder strength to lift with greater power and control.
